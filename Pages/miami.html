Best Things to do in Miami, Florida

PLACES TO SEE

There are so many things to do in Miami, and once you are in the area, you will not know where to start. Have a look at the following and makes sure to add it to your to-do list, they are rated amongst the best places to be in Miami.

  • Miami Beach: This is where all vacations to Miami begin. It is one of Miami's most unrivalled jewel and full of colour and bustling with activities. You probably saw or heard about this in a movie, if you come here you do not want to leave. One of the most fun things to do in Miami!
  • Vizcaya Museum And Gardens: This is located in the Coconut Grove District and here you will be able to find a variety of art from the 15th all the way up to the 19th century. This museum is one of the most famous attractions in Miami.

THINGS TO DO

  • Zoo Miami: The oldest and only tropical zoo in the USA kicked off in 1948 with the purchase of three monkeys, a goat, and to black bears. The zoo continued to slowly acquire animals, and despite a umber of setbacks that included two devastating hurricanes, it became one of the most prominent zoo's in the country.
  • Miami Seaquarium: The oldest oceanarium in the USA is also home to the killer whale Lolita, but the orca shows are just the tip of the iceberg. Here, they'll let you swim with dolphins and seals.
  • Jungle Island Miami: Play and feed the adorable Kangaroos, Capuchins, Lemurs and even a Condor and the celebrity Hercules Tiger.
  • American Airlines Arena: The Arena is a sports and entertainment complex, which hosts numerous sporting events and music concerts. It was constructed in 1998 and is home to the Miami Heat NBA team.
